<h1>Elle's Place</h1>
<br>

<h2>Description</h2>

The site is a web application that provides an easy and efficient way for customers to book reservations for conference rooms. This system streamlines the reservation 
process, allowing users to secure meeting spaces hassle-free.

<h2>Features</h2>

Intuitive Reservation Interface: Easily navigate through the user-friendly interface to view available conference rooms and reserve a time slot.

Real-time Availability: Check real-time availability of conference rooms, ensuring that users can make informed booking decisions.

Flexible Booking Options: Choose from a variety of booking options, including date and time, to accommodate diverse meeting needs.

Reservation Management: View and manage your reservations effortlessly. Cancel or modify bookings with just a few clicks.

User Authentication: Securely log in to the system to access personalized features and manage reservations associated with your account.

<h2>Usage</h2>

Create an Account: Sign up for an account to access reservation management features.

Browse Available Rooms: Explore the available conference rooms, check their features, and select the one that suits your needs.

Make a Reservation: Choose the date, time, and duration for your reservation. Confirm your booking to secure the conference room.

Manage Reservations: View, modify, or cancel your reservations through the user dashboard.
